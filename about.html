About Admission Ac

Today it is one of the most innovative organizations in overseas education consultancy in Bangladesh and acts as a globally recognized bridge between high-quality educational institutions in different parts of the world. For the past five years, we have been assisting discerning students in selecting the right universities and programs for them. Our Core Mission

Making available cutting-edge international education information to all students and helping them towards faster university admission worldwide in line with our quality assurance policy and values.

Our Core Vision

To become one of the most reputed and reliable names worldwide in educational consulting and overseas education service.

Quality Policy

To provide our students with superior services that meet the highest standards of professional performance, satisfy their requirements, and deliver exceptional value. We achieve this by team work and by striving continuously to improve our services. We ensure that our services comply with requirements and meet or exceed student expectations.

Corporate Social Responsibility

The Admission.ac is committed to and bound by its objectives of going beyond business to make a meaningful difference to the communities in which we operate, through education and facilitation of underprivileged and eligible youth. This is the guiding philosophy of the Corporate Social Responsibility initiatives by Admission.ac.

Strategies

To fulfil its mission, The Admission.ac applies five basic strategies:

  1. We offer admission to socially relevant academic program-based institutes abroad which meet the challenging needs of students coming from different backgrounds and seeking different career goals;
  2. We work only for eligible students for admission and visas through free assessment for education providers and immigration rules.
  3. We process admission only for those students who demonstrate the potential to pursue and complete the programs of study they select through a rigorous selection process.
  4. We never deal with bundles of student admissions at a time but work on a case by case basis, which gives proper credential services for both students and education providers.
  5. We practice the principles of good governance that ensure best management practices, best client service and encourage academic freedom and faculty governance.
